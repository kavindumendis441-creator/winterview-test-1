The Residence

Living, quietly elevated.

Winterview was designed as a house first, an apartment second. Each room opens onto the next — a warm kitchen, a sunken living space, bedrooms dressed in soft mustard and denim-blue — all finished with the calm materials of a Galle villa: marble-veined stone, brushed brass, timber-toned tile.

Above it all, a glass-roofed rooftop garden turns the top floor into an open-air room, half indoors, half sky.
